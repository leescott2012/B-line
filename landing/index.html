Coming to the Atlanta BeltLine

The BeltLine finally has a navigation app.

Turn-by-turn directions, how-busy-is-it-right-now info, and the best spots to stop — built for the 22-mile loop, not bolted onto a generic map.

Google Maps treats the BeltLine like a regular street. It's not — it's a 22-mile trail with thousands of people on it every day, and there's never been an app built specifically for walking, running, or biking it.

B-line is that app: real navigation along the trail, a sense of how crowded a segment usually is before you get there, and the restaurants, shops, and dog-friendly spots worth a detour — plus an immersive preview of key segments before you ever step outside.
